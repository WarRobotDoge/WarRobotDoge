Bookworm - Words! Words! Words! This classic vocabulary challenge really knows how to spell f-u-n. Link letters and build words to feed Lex, the insatiable bookworm. Longer words earn Lex's gratitude as well as higher-scoring green, gold and crystal letter tiles. Complete the progressively harder levels to graduate from encyclopedia salesman all the way to bookworm supreme. But don't take too long, as the letters keep piling up ­ and watch out for the fiery red letters that will burn down your library!

About

BookWorm for the BlackBerry was released on April 5, 2007. Similar to other ports of the game, BookWorm for BlackBerry featured its own Settings menu, and a "Scramble" button below the game's main character, Lex the bookworm. BookWorm BlackBerry had 2-3 different versions, but it primarily had a version advertised for the BlackBerry Pearl. Very very little is known about BookWorm for Blackberry, except that it was in the top-ten BlackBerry-enabled games for at least 2 months. A smaller port for BookWorm BlackBerry (Similar to Pocket PC) was sold for 6.99 for many BlackBerry phones at Bplay. One of the BlackBerry ports features the value of each letter by an indicator, which is similar to BookWorm Deluxe. I looked everywhere for a download for it, but the only download links I could find were outdated download links from 2007, or unaccessable Arabic, Russian, and Vietnamese pages. There's only a handful of screenshots that prove this game's existence. If anyone has a download for this, please let me know!
