WarRobotDoge, or simply Doge, is an American YouTuber and political activist known for his videos of the Minecraft server Mineplex, and a vast amount of political content. His channel gained popularity after posting gameplay videos of the game War Robots.
WarRobotDoge has been playing Minecraft for over 25,000 hours, with 2,500 of those hours on Mineplex alone. He is currently at level 87 on Mineplex. WarRobotDoge also has a very large amount of playtime on other games, these include: 500+ hours of CS:GO, 300+ hours on GTA V, 500+ hours on various Resident Evil games, and 5000+ hours on Rocket League.
WarRobotDoge started playing Minecraft in 2011, and Mineplex in late December 2013. He started uploading videos in mid 2017 - early 2018, despite never using the WarRobotDoge pen name. WarRobotDoge has claimed to have had other YouTube channels with subscriber counts ranging from 5,000 to 50,000- though, skeptics do not believe that claim. Likewise, WarRobotDoge has openly been growing other YouTube Channels under at least 5 aliases. WarRobotDoge's pronouns are Handsome/Brilliant. WarRobotDoge is literally Patrick Bateman.
